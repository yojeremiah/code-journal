Pig Dice Game Rules

The rules of the pig dice game are rather simple

A player rolls two dice by clicking the Roll Dice button below

The player can choose to hold their current score, which will add it to their global score and end their turn

The player can also choose to roll the dice again, adding each of their consecutive rolls to the sum of all previous rolls made on that turn

However, the player's current score is reset to zero and their turn is over when they roll a 1 on either die or roll consecutive 6's on either die

The first player to reach the desired global score will win the game
(default winning score = 100)
